# FAQ & Onboarding Content

## Welcome to Smetalab v3!

Smetalab helps you manage construction materials, works, and estimates in one place. Here are the first steps to get started.

## 1. Materials Management
- **Q: How do I add my own materials?**
- **A:** Navigate to the "Materials" section and click "Add Material". You can specify name, unit, price, and categories.

## 2. Works & Guides
- **Q: What are "Works"?**
- **A:** Works are activities or assemblies (e.g., "Wall painting"). You can link materials to works to calculate automated costs.

## 3. Creating Estimates
- **Q: How do I create an estimate?**
- **A:** Go to "Estimates", click "New Estimate", and start adding works and materials from your guides. You can group them into phases.

## 4. Sharing & Collaboration
- **Q: Can I share my estimate with a client?**
- **A:** Yes! Click the "Share" button on any estimate to generate a unique link. You can set an expiration date for the link.

## 5. Subscription & Teams
- **Q: How do I invite colleagues?**
- **A:** Go to "Settings" -> "Team" and enter their email address. They will receive an invitation link.
